🎬 Netflix Content Analysis Power BI Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard exploring Netflix's entire content library — covering 8,807 titles from 1925 to 2021 across movies, TV shows, genres, ratings, and countries.
📌 Dashboard Highlights
Global KPIs
8,807 total titles across 18 content ratings, 515 genres, and 749 countries — spanning nearly a century of content (1925–2021).
Movies vs. TV Shows
70% of the library (6.1K) are Movies; 30% (2.7K) are TV Shows — visualized via a donut chart with year-based toggle filters.
Content Ratings Breakdown
Top ratings by title count: TV-MA leads at 3,207, followed by TV-14 (2,160), TV-PG (862), R (799), and PG-13 (490) — catering to both adult and family audiences.
Genre Analysis
Top genres include International Dramas (362), Documentaries (359), Stand-Up Comedy (334), and Comedies/Dramas (274) — displayed in a scrollable ranked bar chart.
Historical Growth Timeline
Area chart tracing content additions from 1920 to 2021 — showing Netflix's explosive content growth starting around 2015, peaking near 2019–2020.
Year Filters
Toggle buttons for years 2008–2021 plus Movie/TV Show type filters for focused exploration.
✨ Features

Iconic Netflix black & red theme throughout
Year toggle buttons and content-type filters
Donut chart for Movie vs. TV Show split
Area chart for historical release trends
Ranked bar charts for ratings and genres
KPI cards styled to match Netflix branding

🛠️ Tools & Tech

Power BI Desktop
DAX (count measures, filtering, date intelligence)
Custom theme matching Netflix's visual identity

📁 Dataset
Based on the popular Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset (available on Kaggle) — containing title, type, genre, rating, country, release year, and date added fields.
